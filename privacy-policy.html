Privacy Policy

天灵阁 · Your privacy matters

Last updated: June 21, 2026

✦ In Short

We do not collect, store, or share your personal data. No sign-up required. No tracking cookies. Your birth data is used only for the reading you requested and is never saved.

Information We Process (Not Store)

When you use our Destiny Reading or Dream Decoding tools, the birth data you enter (year, month, day, hour, gender) is used exclusively for the real-time calculation of your star chart. This data is processed in memory on our servers and is never written to disk, logged, or stored. Once your reading is generated, the input data is discarded.

No Sign-Up Required

Tianling Pavilion does not require any account creation, email registration, or login. All readings and articles are accessible without providing any personal information.

Cookies

We use no tracking cookies, analytics cookies, or third-party tracking scripts. Our site may use essential functional cookies for Cloudflare Pages delivery optimization, but these do not identify you personally.

Google AdSense

We display advertisements through Google AdSense. Google may use cookies to serve personalized ads based on your browsing history. You can learn more about Google's privacy practices at policies.google.com/privacy. We do not control or have access to any data collected by Google's ad services.

Third-Party Services

Our site is hosted on Cloudflare Pages. Cloudflare may process minimal connection metadata (IP address, browser type) for network routing and security purposes, subject to Cloudflare's Privacy Policy.

Data Security

All connections to our site are served over HTTPS. Your birth data readings are transmitted securely and processed without persistence. We implement industry-standard security measures to protect against unauthorized access.

Your Rights

Since we do not store any personal data, there is nothing to access, correct, or delete. If you have concerns about how your data is handled, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
