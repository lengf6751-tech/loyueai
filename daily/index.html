✋ Palm Reading — Today's Line

Xu Fu traces the rivers of your palm

🖐️Today's Line: The Life Line

Xu Fu turns attention to the Life Line — the curved crease arcing from between thumb and index finger down toward the wrist. Contrary to popular myth, its length does not predict lifespan but rather vitality, major life transitions, and physical resilience. A deep, unbroken Life Line signals robust health and the ability to bounce back from setbacks quickly. A chain-like pattern at the start suggests a delicate childhood that forged your inner steel. Islands (small circles) along the line mark periods of exhaustion — if you spot one, schedule an extra hour of rest this evening. A sister line (a parallel lighter line running alongside) is a rare and auspicious mark: it means a guardian figure — visible or spiritual — walks beside you. Self-check: trace your Life Line slowly and feel for temperature changes along its path; warmth signals active vitality, coolness suggests energy reserves calling for replenishment.
