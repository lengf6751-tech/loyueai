Feng Shui Tips: 25 Quick Energy Fixes for Wealth, Love & Harmony

Updated June 2026 · 6 min read

You don't need to renovate your house or hire a Feng Shui master to shift your energy. These 25 quick fixes — each taking under 10 minutes — come from classical Form School and Compass School Feng Shui. Apply even three of them today and you'll feel the difference.

🚪 Front Door (5 Tips)

The front door is the Mouth of Qi — where all opportunity enters. Start here.

  1. Paint your door the right color. South-facing = red or green. North-facing = black or blue. East = green. West = white or gray. Full door color guide →
  2. Clear the path. Remove bikes, trash bins, dead plants, and clutter from the approach to your door. Qi needs an unobstructed runway.
  3. Fix squeaky hinges. A door that groans when opened "complains" about every opportunity entering. Oil it today.
  4. Place three Chinese coins under the mat. Tied with red ribbon. The oldest and simplest wealth cure.
  5. Light the entrance. Two warm lights flanking the door. A dark entrance = invisible to opportunity.

🛏️ Bedroom (5 Tips)

You spend a third of your life here. The bedroom governs love, rest, and subconscious wealth.

  1. Move your bed to the Commanding Position. Diagonal to the door, solid wall behind you, clear view of the entrance. Full bedroom guide →
  2. Cover or remove mirrors facing the bed. The #1 bedroom mistake. Mirrors reflect restless energy and invite "third party" energy into relationships.
  3. Get a solid headboard. Wood, not metal. No slats. A headboard symbolizes support in life — without one, you're symbolically unprotected.
  4. Remove electronics. No TV, no phone charging on the nightstand. Phone across the room, face-down, airplane mode.
  5. Add a pair of everything. Two nightstands, two lamps, two pillows per person. Singles signal "alone." Full love guide →

💰 Wealth (5 Tips)

The Southeast corner governs prosperity. Activate it.

  1. Find your wealth corner. Stand at the front door, face inward. The far-left corner of your home = Southeast wealth zone. Full wealth guide →
  2. Place a jade plant there. Round leaves = round coins. Alive and thriving is essential — a dying plant repels wealth.
  3. Add something purple. Amethyst crystal, purple cushion, purple flowers. Purple is the ultimate wealth color in Feng Shui.
  4. Keep your stove clean. The stove represents your career income. Clean all burners and rotate usage. Broken burner = blocked income stream.
  5. Fix anything leaking. Dripping faucet = money slowly draining away. This is literal and symbolic.

💼 Office Desk (5 Tips)

Your desk is your career altar. Treat it accordingly.

  1. Face the door. Commanding Position for your desk: see who enters without being directly in line. Back to the door = "stabbed in the back." Full desk guide →
  2. Solid wall behind you. No windows, no open space. The Black Tortoise mountain supports your career.
  3. Place citrine on the far-left corner. The "Merchant's Stone" attracts wealth and prevents money from leaving.
  4. Clear your desk at day's end. Clutter = stagnant Qi = stagnant career. End every day with a clean surface.
  5. Warm task lamp on your non-dominant side. Light = Fire = recognition. Never work under harsh fluorescent alone.

🛋️ Living Room (5 Tips)

The heart of the home — where family Qi circulates.

  1. Arrange seating in a circle. Sofa and chairs should face each other, not all against the walls. Qi circulates in circles. Full living room guide →
  2. Hide the TV when not in use. Inside a cabinet or behind doors. A black mirror shouldn't be your room's focal point.
  3. Keep the center open. No coffee table blocking the center of the room. Qi needs a clear path through the heart of the space.
  4. Add all five elements. Wood (plant), Fire (candle/lamp), Earth (ceramic), Metal (frame), Water (dark blue accent). Balance is key.
  5. Declutter 27 items. 27 = 3 × 9, the emperor's number. Removing 27 unused items from your living room shifts the entire household's energy.
