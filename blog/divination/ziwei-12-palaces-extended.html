The 12 Palaces of Ziwei Dou Shu: Your Complete Life Map

Published May 29, 2026 | Tianling Pavilion

The Twelve Dimensions of Human Life

Ziwei Dou Shu divides human experience into twelve palaces, each governing a specific life domain. Unlike Western astrology which focuses primarily on personality and relationships, the Ziwei system provides a complete map of every significant area of your life — from your innate character to your financial destiny, from your romantic partnerships to your final years.\n\nThe twelve palaces are not separate compartments but interconnected rooms in the mansion of your destiny. Stars in one palace cast their influence into neighboring palaces. The Life Palace sets the fundamental tone that colors every other palace. Understanding all twelve as an integrated whole is the essence of skilled Ziwei interpretation.

The Self Palaces: Who You Are

The Life Palace governs your fundamental nature — your personality, your physical constitution, and the overarching theme of your life journey. It is the most important palace because its ruling star colors every other palace. A Ziwei-ruled Life Palace produces a natural leader regardless of career or relationship configurations.\n\nThe Siblings Palace governs relationships with siblings, close cousins, and peers of your generation. It also indicates your capacity for collaboration and your experience of sibling-like bonds in friendship and work. A strong Siblings Palace suggests natural teamwork ability; a challenged one suggests independence may serve you better.\n\nThe Spouse Palace governs not just your spouse but all significant romantic partnerships. It describes the type of partner you attract, the quality of your marriage, and the timing of significant relationship events. For those unmarried, it describes their approach to romantic partnership.\n\nThe Children Palace governs offspring, creative output, and your relationship with younger generations. It also surprisingly governs your sexual vitality and capacity for pleasure. A strong Children Palace indicates not just fertile family life but also a life rich in creative projects and enjoyments.

The Fortune Palaces: What You Have

The Wealth Palace governs your relationship with money — earning capacity, spending patterns, and the overall arc of your financial life. It reveals not just whether you will be wealthy but HOW wealth comes to you: through career, inheritance, partnership, or investment.\n\nThe Health Palace governs physical well-being, medical predispositions, and the body's resilience. It reveals which organ systems require attention and at which life stages health challenges may arise. A skilled reading of this palace provides preventive guidance decades in advance.\n\nThe Travel Palace governs physical relocation, life transitions, and your relationship with the external world beyond your immediate environment. It indicates whether you will stay rooted in one place or move frequently, and whether travel will benefit or complicate your life.\n\nThe Friends Palace governs your social circle, professional network, and the quality of people you attract. It indicates whether you thrive through broad social networks or deep one-on-one connections, and warns of periods when friends may become sources of difficulty.\n\nThe Career Palace governs professional destiny, public standing, and your relationship with authority and achievement. It reveals not just what career suits you but at what age your career will peak and how to navigate professional transitions.

The Foundation Palaces: Where You Come From

The Property Palace governs real estate, your living environment, and the quality of home life. It reveals whether you will own property, the nature of your ideal home, and how your living situation evolves across life stages.\n\nThe Fortune Palace governs spiritual blessings, karmic merit, and your capacity for contentment. It is the most mysterious palace — it describes the invisible support and unseen forces that shape your life. A strong Fortune Palace can compensate for weaknesses in other palaces.\n\nThe Parents Palace governs your relationship with parents, authority figures, and your ancestral lineage. It describes the quality of support you received in childhood and your relationship with mentors throughout life. It also surprisingly governs your own face and physical appearance.
