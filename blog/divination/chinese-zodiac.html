The 12 Chinese Zodiac Animals: Personality, Fortune, and Compatibility

Divination · Zodiac · 5 min read

The Chinese zodiac (生肖, Shengxiao) is one of the world's oldest astrological systems, dating back over 2,000 years. Unlike Western astrology's monthly cycle, the Chinese zodiac follows a 12-year lunar cycle, with each year represented by an animal sign that influences the personality and fortune of those born under it.

The 12 Animals at a Glance

Rat (鼠) — Quick-Witted and Resourceful

Birth years: 1924, 1936, 1948, 1960, 1972, 1984, 1996, 2008, 2020. Rats are intelligent, adaptable, and socially skilled. They excel in careers requiring strategy and networking. Best matches: Ox, Dragon, Monkey.

Ox (牛) — Diligent and Dependable

Birth years: 1925, 1937, 1949, 1961, 1973, 1985, 1997, 2009, 2021. Oxen are patient, methodical, and fiercely loyal. They build success through sustained effort. Best matches: Rat, Snake, Rooster.

Tiger (虎) — Brave and Competitive

Birth years: 1926, 1938, 1950, 1962, 1974, 1986, 1998, 2010, 2022. Tigers are natural leaders — bold, charismatic, and unpredictable. They thrive in high-stakes environments. Best matches: Horse, Dragon, Dog.

Rabbit (兔) — Gentle and Elegant

Birth years: 1927, 1939, 1951, 1963, 1975, 1987, 1999, 2011, 2023. Rabbits are diplomatic, compassionate, and refined. They prefer harmony over conflict. Best matches: Sheep, Dog, Pig.

Dragon (龙) — Confident and Ambitious

Birth years: 1928, 1940, 1952, 1964, 1976, 1988, 2000, 2012, 2024. The most powerful sign. Dragons radiate authority and magnetism. They are born for greatness. Best matches: Rat, Tiger, Snake.

Snake (蛇) — Wise and Philosophical

Birth years: 1929, 1941, 1953, 1965, 1977, 1989, 2001, 2013, 2025. Snakes possess deep intuition and intellectual depth. They excel in research and strategy. Best matches: Ox, Dragon, Rooster.

Horse (马) — Energetic and Independent

Birth years: 1930, 1942, 1954, 1966, 1978, 1990, 2002, 2014, 2026. Horses crave freedom and adventure. They are persuasive and quick-thinking. Best matches: Tiger, Sheep, Dog.

Sheep (羊) — Creative and Caring

Birth years: 1931, 1943, 1955, 1967, 1979, 1991, 2003, 2015, 2027. Sheep are artistic, empathetic, and gentle. They flourish in supportive environments. Best matches: Rabbit, Horse, Pig.

Monkey (猴) — Clever and Versatile

Birth years: 1932, 1944, 1956, 1968, 1980, 1992, 2004, 2016, 2028. Monkeys are inventive, witty, and endlessly curious. They solve problems others cannot. Best matches: Rat, Dragon.

Rooster (鸡) — Observant and Honest

Birth years: 1933, 1945, 1957, 1969, 1981, 1993, 2005, 2017, 2029. Roosters are precise, confident, and straightforward. They demand excellence. Best matches: Ox, Dragon, Snake.

Dog (狗) — Loyal and Protective

Birth years: 1934, 1946, 1958, 1970, 1982, 1994, 2006, 2018, 2030. Dogs are faithful, honest, and deeply principled. They make steadfast partners. Best matches: Tiger, Rabbit, Horse.

Pig (猪) — Generous and Compassionate

Birth years: 1935, 1947, 1959, 1971, 1983, 1995, 2007, 2019, 2031. Pigs are warm, sincere, and indulgent. They attract abundance through generosity. Best matches: Rabbit, Sheep.
