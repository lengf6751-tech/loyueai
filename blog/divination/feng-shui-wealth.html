1. Find & Activate Your Wealth Corner

64| 65|
RULE 1 66|

Your wealth corner is the far-left corner of your home or room from the front door. This is the Southeast Bagua zone — the "Prosperity Palace."

67| 68|

How to Find It (30 Seconds):

69|
    70|
  1. Stand at your front door, facing into the house
    2. 71|
  2. The far-left corner of the entire home = your Wealth Corner (Southeast)
    3. 72|
  3. This also applies per room — the far-left corner of your living room, office, or bedroom is that room's wealth zone
    4. 73|
74| 75|

What Belongs in Your Wealth Corner:

76|
    77|
  • ✅ Living plants (Wood element feeds wealth)
    • 78|
  • ✅ Purple items — amethyst, purple cloth, purple flowers
    • 79|
  • ✅ A small water feature or image of flowing water
    • 80|
  • ✅ Gold, green, or purple objects
    • 81|
  • ✅ A wealth bowl — a small wooden or ceramic bowl filled with coins, crystals, and rice
    • 82|
83| 84|

What Must NEVER Be in Your Wealth Corner:

85|
    86|
  • ❌ Trash can, recycling bin, or litter box
    • 87|
  • ❌ Bathroom or toilet (if your bathroom is here, keep the door closed and lid down)
    • 88|
  • ❌ Dead plants, clutter, broken objects
    • 89|
  • ❌ Fireplace or stove (Fire destroys Wood, the wealth element)
    • 90|
91| 92|
💡 Bathroom in Wealth Corner? This is common in modern apartments. Keep the door always closed, toilet lid down, and hang a full-length mirror on the outside of the bathroom door. This symbolically "makes the bathroom disappear" from the wealth zone.

5. Water Features for Wealth — The Right Way

151| 152|
RULE 5 153|

A small fountain in the Southeast corner attracts wealth — but ONLY if the water flows toward the center of the home, and NEVER in the bedroom.

154| 155|

Water represents money in Feng Shui. But how the water moves determines whether you attract wealth or watch it drain away.

156| 157|

Water Feature Rules:

158|
    159|
  • ✅ Flow direction: Water must flow toward the interior of your home, not the door
    • 160|
  • ✅ Clean water only: Stagnant or dirty water = stagnant finances. Change water weekly
    • 161|
  • ✅ Best location: Southeast (wealth corner) or North (career)
    • 162|
  • ✅ Size: Small tabletop fountain is ideal; a pond is overkill
    • 163|
  • ❌ Never in the bedroom: Water drowns rest and washes wealth away while you sleep
    • 164|
  • ❌ Never under a staircase: Symbolizes wealth being "pressed down"
    • 165|
166| 167|
💡 No room for a fountain? Hang a painting or photograph of calm, flowing water (river, not ocean or waterfall) in the Southeast corner. The symbolic presence is effective on its own.

6. Chinese Coins & The Three-Legged Toad

172| 173|
RULE 6 174|

Three Chinese coins tied with red ribbon are the oldest Feng Shui wealth cure. Place them in your wallet, under your welcome mat, or in your wealth corner.

175| 176|

The Chinese Coin Cure (3, 6, or 9 coins):

177|
    178|
  • 3 coins tied with red ribbon — placed under the welcome mat, in your wallet, or in the cash register. Attracts wealth from three sources: heaven, earth, and mankind.
    • 179|
  • 6 coins — placed in the wealth corner. Six is the number of "smooth flow" in Chinese numerology.
    • 180|
  • 9 coins — hung near the front door or on a wall facing the entrance. Nine is the emperor's number: ultimate abundance.
    • 181|
182| 183|

The Three-Legged Toad (金蟾):

184|

The Jin Chan (money toad) is the most famous Feng Shui wealth symbol. A three-legged toad with a coin in its mouth, sitting on a bed of coins. Placement rules:

185|
    186|
  • ✅ Facing into the room from near the front door — spitting wealth inward
    • 187|
  • ✅ On a low table or the floor — never above eye level
    • 188|
  • ❌ Never facing the door — spits wealth out
    • 189|
  • ❌ Never in the bathroom, kitchen, or bedroom
    • 190|

7. Your Stove Is Your Wealth Source

195| 196|
RULE 7 197|

In Feng Shui, the stove represents your career income. Keep it spotless, and use all burners equally.

198| 199|

The kitchen stove is the Fire element that "cooks" your wealth. A dirty, neglected stove signals neglected finances. A stove where only one burner is ever used signals limited income streams.

200| 201|

Stove Wealth Rules:

202|
    203|
  • ✅ Keep all burners clean and functional — broken burners = blocked income
    • 204|
  • ✅ Rotate which burner you use — sends the message that income comes from multiple sources
    • 205|
  • ✅ Place a mirror behind the stove (if possible) — "doubles" the burners and income
    • 206|
  • ❌ Never place the stove directly facing the front door — wealth "burns away" before entering
    • 207|
  • ❌ Never place stove next to or facing the sink — Fire and Water clash, extinguishing wealth
    • 208|

8. Wallet Feng Shui — What You Carry Matters

213| 214|
RULE 8 215|

Your wallet is your portable wealth corner. Treat it with the same respect as your home's prosperity zone.

216| 217|

Wallet Rules for Wealth:

218|
    219|
  • ✅ Color: Black, brown, green, gold, or purple. Red is acceptable but "burns" through money faster.
    • 220|
  • ✅ Organization: Bills flat, facing the same direction, arranged by denomination
    • 221|
  • ✅ Always carry some cash — an empty wallet signals "empty finances" to the universe
    • 222|
  • ✅ Place a citrine chip or Chinese coin inside — a micro wealth charm
    • 223|
  • ❌ Never torn, stained, or broken — replace immediately
    • 224|
  • ❌ Never on the floor — money never belongs underfoot
    • 225|
  • ❌ Receipt clutter — old receipts are spent money; clear them weekly
    • 226|

9. Why Clutter Kills Wealth Energy

231| 232|
RULE 9 233|

Clutter is stagnant Qi. Stagnant Qi = stagnant money. Every item you don't use is blocking the flow of new abundance.

234| 235|

This is the most overlooked wealth principle. You can have all the right crystals and plants — but if your home is cluttered, Qi cannot circulate, and wealth cannot reach you.

236| 237|

The 3 Zones That Must Be Clear:

238|
    239|
  1. Front entrance — where Qi first enters. Clutter here blocks all incoming energy
    2. 240|
  2. Wealth corner — clutter in the Southeast "buries" your money
    3. 241|
  3. Under your bed — what you sleep over, you carry in your subconscious finances
    4. 242|
243| 244|

Declutter action: Remove 27 items from your home this week. 27 is 3 × 9 — triple the emperor's number. This symbolic act alone shifts wealth energy.

249| 250|
RULE 10 251|

Activate your wealth corner with a monthly ritual: clear the space, place fresh offerings, and speak your intentions aloud.

252| 253|

Monthly Wealth Corner Ritual:

  3. Light a candle (green or gold) and speak your wealth intention aloud three times
    4. 258|
  4. Leave an offering: A small bowl of rice (symbolizes abundance) or a fresh coin placed daily
    5. 259|

    6. 260|
