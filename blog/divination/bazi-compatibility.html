1. How Bazi Compatibility Works

CORE

Bazi compatibility reads the Five Element interactions between two people's Day Masters — plus how their charts support or challenge each other's Lucky Element.

In Bazi, your Day Master (the Heavenly Stem of your Day Pillar) represents your core self. When two people's Day Masters interact through the Five Element cycles, you get a fundamental compatibility reading:

  • Producing cycle (生): One element generates the other. Person A naturally supports Person B. This is the most harmonious relationship.
  • Controlling cycle (克): One element controls the other. Creates tension — but can be magnetic and passionate.
  • Same element (同): Two people share the same Day Master. Deep understanding, but risk of competition.

Beyond Day Master, full compatibility also looks at: the partner's Lucky Element (does your chart provide it?), Earthly Branch interactions in the Year and Day Pillars, and the 10-Year Luck Cycle overlap.

5. Beyond Day Master: What Else Matters

Day Master compatibility is just the starting point. A full Bazi compatibility reading also examines:

  • Lucky Element match: Does your chart provide your partner's Lucky Element? This is more important than Day Master compatibility. If your chart is rich in your partner's Yong Shen, you are literally their "lucky person."
  • Earthly Branch combinations: The Year Pillar's Branch and Day Pillar's Branch can form Six Harmonies (六合) or Three Unions (三合) — extremely auspicious for marriage.
  • Clashes (冲): If your Day Branch clashes with your partner's (e.g., 子 vs 午, Rat vs Horse), expect fundamental tension. Not fatal, but requires conscious navigation.
  • 10-Year Luck alignment: Are both partners entering favorable luck cycles at similar times? Misaligned cycles can pull couples apart.
