About Tianling Pavilion

天灵阁 · Ancient Wisdom × Modern AI

Tianling Pavilion (天灵阁) was created to bridge the gap between ancient Chinese metaphysical wisdom and the modern world. We believe that systems like Ziwei Dou Shu, Bazi Four Pillars, Qimen Dunjia, and Zhou Gong dream interpretation — refined over thousands of years — are too valuable to be locked away in obscure manuscripts or behind expensive consultation fees.

✦ Our Mission

To make authentic Chinese metaphysics accessible to everyone. Not watered-down horoscopes or vague fortune cookie predictions — real star charts calculated by proven algorithms, genuine Bazi analysis grounded in Five Element theory, and dream interpretation rooted in the Zhou Gong tradition.

How We Work

Every reading on Tianling Pavilion combines three independent systems — Ziwei, Bazi, and Qimen — calculated in real time using open-source algorithms trusted by the Chinese metaphysics community. The Scribe (Xu Xun) then synthesizes all three readings into one coherent oracle. Each result is completely free, with no sign-up required.

Our Knowledge Base contains over 100 free articles covering Chinese zodiac, face reading, palm reading, Feng Shui, dream interpretation, and more — all written in plain language that anyone can understand.

Our Technology

We use the iztro open-source algorithm for Ziwei Dou Shu calculations, combined with rule-based Bazi analysis based on authentic Daoist principles. All processing happens on our servers — no data is stored or shared. Your privacy is built into our architecture from the ground up.
